Community

Be part of the LearnEnglish community

We run live, online, teacher-led events where you can listen, learn and practise with other learners. Get help from our online community to help you reach your goals.

LearnEnglish webinars

LearnEnglish webinars are 30-minute webinars given by our content team – all expert teachers with many years' experience. Our webinars are created for learners at different levels and will help you improve your English.

Community events

The British Council organises events on social media where you can ask our expert educators questions. If you have any questions about our courses or about learning English in general, you're welcome to join our live events.
